Google

Birth Of ARPA TCP/IP Email World Wide Web Web Browser Google

Google was officially launched in 1998 by Larry page and Sergey Brin to market google search which was become the most use the web based search engine. Larry Page and Sergey Brin, students at Stanford University in California, developed a search algorithm at first known as BackRub in 1996, with the help of Scott Hassan and Alan Steremberg. The domain google.com was registered on September 15, 1997. The search engine soon proved successful and the expanding company moved several times. In 2000 google began selling advertisements associated with such keywords. Microsoft had been touting its Bing search engine to counter Google's competitive position.

With the dotcom collapse still going strong, Wikipedia launched in 2001, one of the websites that paved the way for collective web content generation/social media.

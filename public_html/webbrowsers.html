WEB BROWSER

Birth Of ARPA TCP/IP Email World Wide Web Web Browser Google

Marc Andreessen and Eric Bina - two programmers working at NCSA, developed a sophisticated browser that they called Mosaic in 1992. Mosaic was a user-friendly browser which helped to search the web to see the words and pictures on the same page for the first time and navigate using scroll bars and clickable links. Name Mosaic later changed as Netscape.

Yahoo who was started in 1994 by Jerri Yang and David Filo in Stanford University. It grew rapidly throughout 1990s and diversified into a web portal. By the end of 1994 Yahoo has already received 1 million hits. On March 8, 1997 Yahoo acquired online communications company Four11 so their webmail service rocket Mail became Yahoo! Mail. On March 8, 1998 Yahoo launched Yahoo Pager and instant messaging service that was renamed yahoo messenger a year later. It provides a web portal, search engine Yahoo Search, and related services, including My Yahoo!, Yahoo Mail, Yahoo News, Yahoo Finance, Yahoo Sports and its advertising platform, Yahoo! Native.
